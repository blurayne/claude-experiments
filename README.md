# Claude Experiments

A collection of small, self-contained experiments. Each subfolder is one experiment with its own `index.md` (or hand-written `index.html`).

**Live site:** [https://blurayne.github.io/claude-experiments/](https://blurayne.github.io/claude-experiments/)

## Experiments

- [microbes-overview](microbes-overview/) — Bilingual (DE/EN) print-ready posters that visualise common microbes, generated from a Python data file into HTML and PDF.
- [solar-system](solar-system/) — Interactive 3D viewer of the solar system rendered in the browser.
- [slothy-hutty](slothy-hutty/) — Procedural 3D "Slothy" character rendered by hand-written WebGL2 and WebGPU engines, with a face-changer, cel/smooth shading, and 3D-print exports (STL / 3MF / OBJ).
- [steampipe-lightning-talk](steampipe-lightning-talk/) — A 3–5 minute reveal.js slide deck about Steampipe, with Kubernetes / AWS / aggregator / JSON examples and a DuckDB end note.
- [jq-lightning-talk](jq-lightning-talk/) — A reveal.js slide deck about jq, its Turing-completeness, and the family of alternative implementations (gojq, jaq, fq, faq).
- [esp32-iot-lightning-talk](esp32-iot-lightning-talk/) — A 5-minute reveal.js slide deck on using an ESP32 as an IoT sensor, shipping data to InfluxDB, and shipping firmware with OTA updates.
- [terragrunt](terragrunt/) — A 10-minute reveal.js lightning talk on Terragrunt vs Terraform: units vs modules, DRY backend config, the dependency DAG, `run --all`, stacks, what Terragrunt 1.0 finished after ~10 years, and a quick Atmos comparison.
- [climate-report](climate-report/) — Interactive climate-trend dashboard for Munich (warming trend with extrapolation to 2036, extremes, monthly heatmap, precipitation/sun/UV), built from real LMU and DWD data.
- [gebrauchtwagen-kompass](gebrauchtwagen-kompass/) — Interactive used-car comparison dashboard (German) in a marine-compass theme: speed-dependent fuel curves, total cost of ownership, reliability dots and scale silhouettes.
- [palestine-my-ass](palestine-my-ass/report.html) — Interactive German fact-check dossier on the Sophie von der Tann controversy: ten criticisms weighed point-by-point against what speaks in her favor, portraits of critics and defenders, a look at the legitimacy of different journalism genres, and an AI red-team experiment comparing how differently-prompted models (and other models like Grok) answer the same question.
- [world-in-numbers](world-in-numbers/) — Live, ticking dashboard of how life on Earth is being born and lost (humans, trees, insects, birds, fish, animals): per-minute born/killed flows, a slider to project totals to any year up to 2100, and study-based trajectories that rise or fall.
- [blood-vessels](blood-vessels/) — Interactive, zoomable microvascular network: procedurally grown arteries → capillaries → veins (space-colonisation or recursive-tree generators) with pulsatile blood flow and switchable shading algorithms (Canvas 2D, plus WebGL2 SDF-capsule Blinn–Phong, subsurface, toon and x-ray). See [`techniques.md`](blood-vessels/techniques.md).
- [eco-navigation](eco-navigation/) — Compares two Bavarian-Forest routes (Deggendorf → Engelshütt, via Arnbruck vs. via Viechtach & Bad Kötzting) for distance, elevation, curviness, speed and — for five cars (Toyota Auris Hybrid, VW ID.3, Fiat Panda, 2005 Opel, Mercedes C-diesel) — energy, cost and CO₂. Geometry & curviness are measured from real GPX tracks; elevation and energy are a transparent physics model. Method, algorithm & data sources: [`methodology.html`](eco-navigation/methodology.html) ([`METHODOLOGY.md`](eco-navigation/METHODOLOGY.md)).
- [putzhilfe_ratgeber](putzhilfe_ratgeber/) — Interaktiver deutscher Ratgeber zum legalen Einstellen einer Putzhilfe in München (Minijob/Haushaltsscheck, Selbstständige, Firmen/Plattformen): Kostenrechner mit Slidern (0,25-h-Schritte, Monatsanzeige, 14,62 % Abgaben 2026, § 35a-Steuerbonus, 603-€-Grenzen-Check), Plattform-Vergleich für München, Sicherheits-, Katzen- und Erstgespräch-Checklisten sowie die Perspektive der Putzhilfe — per Deep-Research mit Quellen belegt (Stand Juli 2026).
- [ocr_mud_run_calendar](ocr_mud_run_calendar/) — Interaktiver OCR- & Mud-Run-Kalender (deutsch) für Hindernis-, Matsch- und Vereins-Crossläufe rund um München (81373) und Lam im Bayerischen Wald (93462): Umschalter für den Ursprungsort, Slider für Reichweite, Zeitraum und max. Startgebühr, Sortierung nach Termin oder Entfernung, Filter für Großevents / kleine Vereinsläufe / Kids-Läufe, pro Event Strecken-Chips und Kostenstaffelung.
- [schlafsofa](schlafsofa/) — Deutscher Ratgeber für Schlafsofas im täglichen Gebrauch (Dauerschläfer) bis 3.500 € Neupreis, per Deep-Research mit Quellen belegt (Juli 2026): ~55 konkrete Modelle in vier Preisklassen inkl. Wohnlandschaften mit Schlaffunktion (IKEA, OTTO/INOSIGN/Jockenhöfer, Roller/POCO/Höffner/XXXLutz, Innovation Living, Bali, Nehl, Sedda, Ligne Roset Multy, franz fertig, Signet …), interaktive Feature-Matrix ([`vergleich.html`](schlafsofa/vergleich.html)) mit Slidern (Neupreis, Gebraucht-Budget, Liegebreite, Alltagstauglichkeit), optionalem Zimmer-Check (einstellbare Wandbreite/Tiefe, persistiert in localStorage) und Checkboxen (Dauerschläfer, echte Matratze, Bettkasten, waschbarer Bezug, Wohnlandschaft …), Kleinanzeigen-Gebrauchtpreise samt Angebotslage, Besichtigungs-Checkliste, Life-Hacks (Topper-Trick & Co.) und Platz-Optionen für kleine Stadtwohnungen.
- [ki-arbeitsmarkt](ki-arbeitsmarkt/) — Adversariales Recherche-Protokoll-Audit (deutsch) zu den Arbeitsmarkt- und Fiskaleffekten generativer KI: vier konkurrierende Hypothesen (Disruption läuft / selektive Umverteilung / Nullhypothese-Hype / fiskalische Asymmetrie), sechzehn atomare Einzelbehauptungen mit Erstquellen-Prüfung, Belegtabelle, Restliste offener Punkte und dreistufigem Verdikt je Hypothese (Stand Juli 2026).
- [termux-claude-remote](termux-claude-remote/) — Design doc for a bidirectional bridge between a phone running Termux and a locked-down cloud sandbox: the sandbox borrows the phone's network via a forward proxy (`HTTPS_PROXY`) while the phone drives the sandbox over a control channel, with both peers dialing out to a rendezvous (Tailscale mesh by default) so carrier-grade NAT on either end stops mattering. Covers connectivity trade-offs, copy-paste Termux/Tailscale/tinyproxy snippets, and a default-deny security model.
- [immune-heroes](immune-heroes/)
- [reise-ins-allerkleinste](reise-ins-allerkleinste/) — German reveal.js slide deck "Die Reise ins Allerkleinste": particle physics from cells to quarks, part of the *Quarks for Kids* series. — **Immune Heroes**: a kids' (DE/EN) browser voice app. Talk out loud to a cast of immune-system friends (Senua, Rubina, Theo, Kilian, Denni, Makro), each with its own voice and character, powered by the OpenAI Realtime API (speech↔speech over WebRTC) with an animated SVG character, audio-reactive visuals, idle story-telling, a token counter and a private on-device memory.
- [ferienprogramm-feuer-kinder](ferienprogramm-feuer-kinder/) — Interactive German "Ferien-Kompass" for the 2026 Bavarian summer holidays (3 Aug–14 Sep): 100+ curated courses, camps and drop-in activities for kids, switchable between Greater Munich (105 offerings from Sendling out to Fürstenfeldbruck, Dachau, Starnberg, Erding, Freising and Ebersberg) and Lam in the Bavarian Forest (93462, 14 offerings) via a location toggle, filterable by a real-distance (haversine) slider, week, activity (robotics/maker, dance, sports, creative, outdoors, science, water, animals/farm, languages), price/free, open slots, age and free-text search, with a print/copy-friendly shortlist tray and an auto/manual light-dark theme toggle.
- [ki-impact](ki-impact/) — Berichte und Analysen zur volkswirtschaftlichen Wirkung von KI; enthält einen interaktiven adversarialen Evidenzbericht zum IWF-Papier über KI, Arbeitsmarkt und Besteuerung.
- [recherche-protokoll](recherche-protokoll/) — Wiederverwendbare Prompt-Vorlage für adversariale Tiefenrecherche zu beliebigen Themen (Kontroversen, Technik-/Produktvergleiche, Studienlagen, Politik-Claims): Hypothesen statt Lagerfrage, Autoritäten-Deklassierung, aktives Widerlegen aller Seiten, Belegtabelle statt rundem Fazit — in Voll-, Kompakt- und Ein-Satz-Version sowie als fertige `CLAUDE.md`/`AGENTS.md`-Instruktionsdatei für andere Projekte.

## How the site is built

GitHub Pages is built by [`.github/workflows/pages.yml`](.github/workflows/pages.yml):

1. The workflow runs `.github/scripts/build_site.py`.
2. Each subfolder is copied verbatim into `_site/`.
3. If a subfolder ships its own `index.html`, that file is kept untouched.
4. Otherwise, `index.md` is rendered into `index.html`.
5. The top-level `README.md` you are reading becomes the site root.

The Pages source must be set to **GitHub Actions** in repository settings.
